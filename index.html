关于我

About Me

张义萌，博士，副研究员，硕士生导师，四川省天府峨眉计划青年人才（原省青年千人），入选教育部海外博士后引才计划。担任国家自然科学基金通讯评审专家，教育部研究生学位论文通讯评议专家，世界交通运输大会青年委员，以及 Transportation Research B/C/E 等期刊审稿人。荷兰代尔夫特理工大学交通工程与物流专业博士毕业，博士后研究方向多式联运及多模式交通优化。

Yimeng Zhang, Ph.D., Associate Researcher, Master Supervisor, Sichuan Tianfu Emei Program Young Talent, selected for the MOE Postdoctoral Talent Program. Reviewer for NSFC, graduate thesis expert, youth committee member of the World Conference on Transport, and reviewer for Transportation Research B/C/E. Ph.D. in Transport and Logistics from Delft University of Technology; postdoctoral research focuses on multimodal transport and optimization.
